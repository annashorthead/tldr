# lynx 

> A Command-line web browser.

- To visit a website:

`lynx {{https://duckduckgo.com}}`

- To move among the links:

`Up (arrow key), Down (arrow key)`

- To follow links:

`Enter or the right (arrow key)`

- To go back to the last page:

`Left (arrow key) or u`

- to print the output to the Terminal:

`-dump`

- Exit:

`q then y`
